About Us

Founding Story:

LUME Group was founded in the early 2010s by Jonathan D. Lumé, a visionary leader with a versatile professional background spanning construction, technology, and entertainment. Recognizing the untapped potential within the media and entertainment sectors, Jonathan sought to create a dynamic investment company dedicated to capitalizing on emerging opportunities while fostering innovation and operational excellence. His extensive experience, from managing logistics in film and TV production to self-directed trading in the media sector, uniquely positioned him to understand the intricate landscape of investment management.

With a mission to leverage diversified portfolios, direct equity investments, and pursue leveraged buyouts, LUME Group emphasizes strategic partnerships and hands-on operational roles to enhance the value of its portfolio. Jonathan’s commitment to ongoing learning and adaptation drives the company’s ability to navigate the complexities of the global market, making LUME Group a leader in identifying and maximizing growth opportunities for its stakeholders.

Early Ventures and Growth:

LUME Group, under the leadership of Jonathan D. Lumé, was founded on a robust foundation of diverse experiences that are crucial for a media and entertainment financier. In its early years, the company engaged in various entrepreneurial ventures honing Jonathan’s ability to manage complex projects and prioritize business needs. These skills are directly transferable to identifying and nurturing owner-focused services in potential acquisition targets, where understanding the nuances of small businesses is paramount.

Transitioning to the film and television industry, LUME Group expanded its operational acumen by providing production assistance support. This role required a keen ability to adapt quickly and solve problems on the fly—qualities that are invaluable in the fast-paced world of self-directed trading and investment management, where flexibility and rapid decision-making can significantly impact successful trades and buyout negotiations.

Jonathan's experiences have cultivated a deep understanding of owner-focused services that are critical for LUME Group’s investment strategy. By focusing on the needs and motivations of business owners, he leverages his project coordination and management skills to create tailored operational plans that enhance value and drive performance improvements in portfolio companies post-acquisition. This owner-centric approach enables LUME Group to build strong relationships with management teams, facilitating smoother transitions and better integration strategies, which ultimately lead to higher returns on investment.

As LUME Group evolved, it emphasized operational excellence through Jonathan's extensive involvement in various roles. His experience in commercial and stagecraft construction, along with roles in warehouse quality control and restoration work, instilled a rigorous attention to detail and a commitment to operational integrity. These qualities are essential for conducting thorough due diligence and understanding the operational levers within target companies that can be optimized for enhanced profitability.

The diverse skill set Jonathan developed through these early ventures positions LUME Group to execute successful media and entertainment investments. The company focuses on identifying underperforming or undervalued businesses, where Jonathan's technical skills and insights into operational efficiencies can be immediately applied. By prioritizing owner-focused services, LUME Group not only enhances the value of its acquisitions and angel investments but also ensures a more sustainable growth trajectory for its portfolio companies.

Jonathan's background and experiences provide LUME Group with a strategic advantage in the media and entertainment space. By understanding the intricacies of business operations and fostering owner-focused relationships, LUME Group is well-equipped to drive value creation, enabling successful outcomes for both the company and its stakeholders.

Recognizing the importance of enhancing internal operations post-acquisition, LUME Group made a strategic pivot towards integrating owner-focused services into its investment strategy.
